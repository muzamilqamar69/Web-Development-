Who I Am

My name is Muhammad Muzamil Qamar. I am passionate about Web Development and I love creating meaningful projects with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.A

What is the Web?

The World Wide Web (WWW), or simply the web, is a system of interlinked documents and applications that are accessed through the internet. It allows people all over the world to share information, communicate, and build applications using technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

My Journey with Web Development

Getting Started

I started learning HTML5 to structure content, and CSS3 to make it visually appealing. My first small project was building a simple personal homepage with text and images.

Expanding Skills

Later, I explored JavaScript to make web pages interactive. I created small projects like a to-do list app and a digital clock. These projects helped me understand how coding brings life to static web pages.

Current Work

Currently, I am focusing on building multi-page websites using semantic HTML5 layouts, forms, tables, video/audio embedding, and applying good design practices. This portfolio is one of the projects I am most proud of.
